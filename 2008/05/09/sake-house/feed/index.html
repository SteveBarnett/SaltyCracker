Mon, 17 Nov 2014 12:19:35 +0000 hourly 1 http://wordpress.org/?v=4.0.8 By: Steve http://saltycracker.co.za/2008/05/09/sake-house/comment-page-1/#comment-11 Thu, 11 Sep 2008 14:35:07 +0000 http://saltycracker.co.za/?p=38#comment-11 Most people like to champion the more expensive Willoughby’s at the waterfront as the best sushi in CT, but Sake House is definitely in my top 5 of joints that do tasty and not too expensive sushi.

Sake House has great sushi, nummy sashimi and a nice range of weird / nouveau / fusion sushi.
I particularly dig the Africa Dream (with springbok carpaccio – mmm…).

The decor is groovy – all dark wood and light colour bamboo type things. Only downside is the telly over the bar which can be distracting.
